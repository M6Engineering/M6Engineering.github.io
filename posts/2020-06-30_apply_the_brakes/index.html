Apply The Brakes!

2020-06-30 :: Alistair English

Today we hit our first key milestone - braking on the kart is now electronically controlled.

We achieved this by attaching two high torque servo motors to the kart’s brake lever. Getting this system working took some extra testing, as we originally thought that one servo would generate enough power to properly apply the brake. However, this was not the case.

One of our successful tests from this afternoon:

Currently the servos are controlled by an RC receiver for ease of prototyping. Servo control of the throttle is part-way done, but more testing is required. Now that our order of servos has arrived, we are also beginning work on a steering system.
